Our Story

Our Story: Tradition, Taste, and Osaka’s Spirit

“At Harukoma, we believe great sushi is more than just a meal—it’s an experience rooted in freshness, generosity, and Osaka’s warm hospitality. For years, we’ve delighted locals and travelers with thick cuts of sushi, authentic flavors, and unbeatable value. Our story is one of passion, tradition, and a deep love for sharing Japanese cuisine that leaves lasting memories.”

Harukoma has long been a favorite in Osaka, where tradition meets generous flavors. Known for fresh seafood and hearty portions of sushi, we continue to serve with the same passion and care that made us a local treasure. Our story is built on authenticity, community, and the joy of sharing unforgettable meals
